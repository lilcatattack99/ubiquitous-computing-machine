This here was preparatory work for a lino piece. Theme here was animals. For this lino project I went with an owl. To do a lino piece you need a sheet of lino, a cutting knife, paint(any colour) and paper any colour of your choice. You trace the image then with the cutting knife, carve out the black or white bits. Depends on your preference. After cutting the piece apply paint to the lino the place the coloured sheet on the top. Rub in the sheet then gently peel it of the lino block. And set to dry.
